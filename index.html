Welcome to The Aqua Pool


Here at The Aqua Pool we have a variety of sessions to suit all. Whether you are looking for a general swim to aqua aerobics, lane swim to inflatable sessions.

Our 25 meter long pool has the capacity for 6 lanes and with our slow, medium and fast lane system you can choose what best suits you and your style.


Benefits of swimming


Regular swimming is proven to reduce your risk of serious illnesses, such as heart disease, type 2 diabetes and stroke. It can also boost your mood and reduce stress levels to improve your mental wellbeing. It can also help you lose weight, improve general flexibility, strength and mobility. There is less strain on your joints when exercising in the water and it is a great form of exercise for people of any age or ability.
